Stellar Sharks

Sharks are one of the most fierce and feared predators that patrol the seas and lurk in the shadows. The perfect apex predator that can grow up to 40 feet long and have 6 inch thick skin. Many sharks go through up to 30,000 teeth in a lifetime that can last anywhere between 20-30 years. The largest shark ever known was 53ft long and had a mouth that opened over 6ft wide.
